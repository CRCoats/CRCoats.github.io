Welcome

My name is Cody and welcome to my project!   My goal for this site was to showcase some of my interests, but since I have so many I settled on the one hobby I've enjoyed the longest: Photography. I've taken a million junk photos and a few shining gems but whatever the outcome I love the idea of capturing the perfect moment and freezing it in time.

I love taking photos of different landscapes and nature. Between my camera and using my phone I always end up taking more photos than I'll ever need, but that just means I have plenty of photos to share! Enjoy looking at some of them and feel free to reach out and connect with me.

About Me

I was born and raised in California but have travelled all over for various reasons. Roadtrips and family visits at different times found me travelling around the U.S. states. Mid-west and east-coast treks always gave me new photo opportunities.

For university I journeyed up to Oregon which has an incredible amount of natural beauty to discover. My time in uni also allowed me the opportunity to travel far abroad, and I found myself in the Middle East traversing Jordan and Israel on a memorable trip.

Travelling over the years has taught me it is always best to share one's experiences and memories and doing so makes them more significant. My hope is for everyone to experience something positive when perusing, so please enjoy yourself!
